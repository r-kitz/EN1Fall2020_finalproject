My Project: Xylophone Player

This robot plays the xylophone! Try to play a few notes to start out.

Once you are ready, create your own song, or try to play one of the challenge songs below!

Challenge Songs:

1. Hot Cross Buns

2. Mary Had a Little Lamb

How to Play:

Enter a sequence of notes (using C, D, E, G, or R (for rest)), and press "Play!" when you are ready to hear your song!
